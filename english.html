what's taiping



Typing Center for learning to type quickly on a computer in Arabic and English.

The trainee has three sessions per week, which they choose.

The course includes three modules, each specializing in a specific area.

The first module: Learning to type quickly on the keyboard in Arabic This module includes fourteen sessions, each covering a new lesson until the trainee masters the complete alphabet, numbers, and symbols.

The second course: The second course is combined with the first course. This means that on each of the three days the trainee attends, they will take a typing lesson. This course consists of three sessions, during which the trainee learns the internal and external parts of the computer and practices practically under the guidance of the center's supervisors.

The third and final course: This course covers Microsoft Word and is the last course to teach the entire Word program. Regards, Typing Center.
